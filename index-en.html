About me

Designer Tariq Riaz traveled a unique path on his way to discovering his love for jewelry design. When he was a student at the University of Arizona, Tucson, the annual influx of gem dealers and enthusiasts that flooded the town each February was just a vague distraction. He never imagined he’d one day be immersed in a world of precious metals and gemstones. While he’d long had hobbies as a maker, he was always analytically minded, which compelled him to begin his professional career in engineering and robotics, solving problems that focused on the integrities and architecture of design. He excelled in his profession, rising up the ranks, marrying his wife, and eventually moving with her to the Abu Dhabi.
